What It Does

This is a tool for creating updated versions of your YouTube playlists. So you can:
  • Watch/listen to a selection of the most recent videos from each playlist
  • Remove any 'dead' videos from a playlist
  • Shuffle the videos in each playlist. (Independently of any future YouTube redesigns)
Note - all your original playlists will remain completely unaltered. So you can replace all those dead videos at your leisure...
